My name is Lina Moon, and I'm an user experience + graphic designer.

Recently, I received the ACP 2014 Design of the Year Award for my work at the UTD Mercury, where I worked as an art director.

Currently, I'm practicing different styles of Frakfurt calligraphy, with the green 3.8mm Pilot Parallel pen as my weapon of choice.
